• North direction is ruled by Lord Kuber(the Lord of Wealth). If the Main door is at the North direction, then it is considered very auspicious and would help to achieve financial gains and growth.

• The Main door should open inwards in a clockwise direction.

• Enter your home with your right leg in first.

• Do not install the symbol of Swastik & Maa Laxmiji Charan on the floor level.

• If you have installed a moorti of Lord Ganesha ji above the Main Door from outside, then install a moorti of Lord Ganesha ji above the Main Door from inside the home also(ie. the moorti/picture of Lord Ganesha ji should be installed back to back).

• Install a light outside the main door and keep it switched on during the evening hours.

•Install the ‘SACRED SYMBOL’ of Om or Swastik above the Entrance Door frame(install the symbols inside and outside).
