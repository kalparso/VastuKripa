About Vastu Kripa App

Vastu Kripa App is an app which is developed to make your Office, House, Factory, etc. in appropriate direction according to the Vastu Shastra.

With help of this app you can make the each and every part of your house, factory, office, etc in correct direction.

How to use the app:

Use this app to start scoring a new house, office, factory, etc.

  • Stand in the center of the house and orient yourself with a fixed direction with the help of the compass. And please put the phone parallel to the earth axis (This suggestion is given by Android Developerâ€™s).
  • Room/Space name is displayed at the top of each page (e.g. Director Cabin). Also you can see the score regarding the direction of the place.
  • You can check the Vastu Tips for the particular place by clicking "Tips for this Direction" button, for particular direction using compass. You can select different Vastu places by clicking "Evaluate other vastu places" button.
