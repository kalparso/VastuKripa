• Above the Main Door frame, Install a picture of Lord Hanumanji(inorder to gain Divine protection from the negativities).

• Install the ‘SACRED SYMBOL’ of Om or Swastik above the Entrance Door frame(install the symbols inside and outside). Avoid sleeping on the metal/wrought iron bed.
