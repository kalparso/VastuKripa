• The 'Tijori'(Vault/Safe)/Cash Drawer should never open towards South or West direction because it could affect the flow of funds and create financial drains.

• The cash drawer/vault(locker) should open towards the Uttar disha(North zone) or towards the Poorav disha(East zone).

• Paint the locker in yellow or off white shade(from inside and outside). Avoid grey, black and blue painted vault(locker).

• Do not keep shoes below the Vault(Tijori).

• The cash drawer/vault(locker) should be never be kept empty. It is always advisable to keep some valuables and cash permanently in it.
