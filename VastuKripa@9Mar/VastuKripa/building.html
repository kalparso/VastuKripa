Points considered by us during the Vastu audit. We examine the plot of land for:

  • Shape of Plot, Dimensions of plot and Ground level.
  • Facing direction of plot (degree of magnetic North).
  • High tension wire & other obstruction (“T” Junction etc.).
  • History of the previous owners.
  • Energy level check of the plot.
  • Compatibility check of the plot with the buyer.
  • Bore well / underground tanks, ETP, Generator.
  • And many other aspects in relation to the property.

After identifying the suitable property, We suggest the ideal layouts (macro level) and internal layout (micro level) of the proposed building and flats (apartments)-

Macro Level:

  • Main Gate, Driveway and Lawns location.
  • Bore-well, Underground tank location.
  • Rain water harvesting pit location.
  • Swimming Pool & Gymnasium location.
  • Transformer, Generator Room, Electric Room location.
  • Drainage slopes, Septik Tank, Soak-pit location.
  • External paint colour schemes.
  • Rectification of the ‘missing / cut corners’ and ‘extended / projected corners’.

Micro Level:

  • Main Door and Windows location.
  • Kitchen location and layout.
  • Toilet location and layout.
  • Master Bed room, Children’s room, Guest room location and layout.
  • Rectification of the ‘missing / cut corners’ and ‘extended / projected corners’.
