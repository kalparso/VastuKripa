• Advisable to create the Director Cabin at the South(Dakshin disha), because this could make the Director powerful, bolder, stronger and decisive.

• Ensure that the Directors face North side(ideally) or North-East or East side.

• Directors and Executives should not sit below the overhead beams or below the overhead cabinets.

• Do not place heavy furniture at the North-East corner of the cabin.

• Avoid keeping unwanted things lying around and too many books lying un-necessarily on the desk.

• Avoid sharp cornered furniture/columns. Do not sit below over-head beams or sharp cornered cabinets or open shelves.

• In order to further empower the Director and to make him focussed and balanced, we suggest to Install our Vastu Product Accessories: ‘POWER GRID STICK’ on the wall(install it in the corresponding wall behind the Director Seat at about 4 feet level or above.).

• General attributes of South zone(Dakshin disha):
South Ruled by: Lord Yama.
South Ruled by: Mars planet(Mangal).
Color: Red, Deep Maroon.
Mantra to be recited: 'Om Kram Krim Kroom Sah Bhomaey Namah'.
Gemstone: Red Coral(Moonga/Mangal).
