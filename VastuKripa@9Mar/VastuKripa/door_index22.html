• North-East direction is the 'Dev Moola'(zone of the Gods and Goddesses). If the Main door is at the North-East direction, then it is considered very auspicious and beneficial in all spheres of life ie. health, wealth and relationships.

• Ensure that any picture/statue of Lord Ganesha ji does not back your house. Always ensure that the picture/statue of Lord Ganesha ji is installed back to back.

• The Main door should open inwards in a clockwise direction.

• Enter your home with your right leg in first.

• If you have installed a moorti of Lord Ganesha ji above the Main Door from outside, then install a moorti of Lord Ganesha ji above the Main Door from inside the home also(ie. the moorti/picture of Lord Ganesha ji should be installed back to back).

• Install a light outside the main door and keep it switched on during the evening hours.

• Install the ‘SACRED SYMBOL’ of Om or Swastik above the Entrance Door frame(install the symbols inside and outside).

• General attributes of North-East zone(Eshanya Kona, Dev Moola):
North-East Ruled by: Eshanya(the Supreme Lord).
North-East Ruled by: Jupiter Planet(Brihaspati).
Color: Yellow/Golden.
Mantra to be recited: 'Om Gram Grim Grom Sah Guruveh Namah'.
Gemstone: Yellow Sapphire(Pukhraj).
Element: Water(Jal Tatva)
