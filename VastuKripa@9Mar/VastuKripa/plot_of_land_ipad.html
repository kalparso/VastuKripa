Buying or developing a Plot of Land as per Vastushastra would help the Land Developer achieve:

  • Helps the Developer to achieve smooth and faster completion of the project.
  • The Developer can expect quick sales of the properties and better profitability.
  • The Developer would have better co-ordination with PMC, Govt. Departments, Architects, contractors, labour etc.
  • Improved inflow of money and stability of finances for the Developer.
  • Opens up doors for prosperity and success for the Developer and the Occupants.

Points considered by us during the Vastu audit. We examine the plot of land for:

  • Shape of Plot, Dimensions, extended corners, missing corners (reductions).
  • Ground level and topography of the plot.
  • Facing direction of plot (degree of magnetic North).
  • High tension wire & other obstruction (“T” Junction etc.).
  • History of the previous owners.
  • Colour of the Soil.
  • Physical appearance of the Land.
  • Energy Level (Cosmic, Global, Telluric Energies).
  • Geopathic Stress.
  • Water features, lake, water stream, ‘nala’ etc.
  • Compatibility Test with “Dowsing” technique.
  • Bore well / underground tanks, ETP, Generator etc.
  • Constructed building (if any).
  • And many other aspects in relation to the plot.
  • Main Door & Main Gate location.
  • Driveway and boundary walls.
  • Bore-well, Underground tank location.
  • Rain water harvesting pit location.
