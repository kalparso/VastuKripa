• Do not keep multiple temples/statues/pictures in different zones, hence create a single temple in the premises.

• The photos and idols of God should be installed on the Poorav disha(East side). The devotee performing Puja should face Poorav disha(East side) or God's photo and moorti should face Poorav disha(East side).

• Play \"Vastu Shanti\" and other mantras in a daily routine.

• Do not keep pictures of departed souls(ancestors) in the Temple and especially on the same platform with other pictures of God. Ideally the pictures of departed souls should be installed on the Dakshin walls.

• Install the ‘DIVINE CONNECT VEDIC YANTRA FRAME’ (depicting ShreeYantra, Lord Vastu Devta Vedic Yantra, Lord Kuber Vedic Yantra and Nav Grah Vedic Yantra) in the temple and recite the mantras and worship regularly.

• Do not use black, grey granite in the temple. Ideally use white, beige or green marble.

• General attributes of North zone(Uttar disha):
North Ruled by: Lord Kuber(The Lord of Wealth) & Maa Durga.
North Ruled by: Mercury Planet(Budh).
Color: Green.
Mantra to be recited: 'Om Bram Brim Broom Sah Budhaey Namah'.
Gemstone: Green Emerald(Panna).
