• Kitchen should not be constructed at the Uttar disha(North direction). Ideally, the kitchen should be in the Agnisthaan(South-East zone) of the home. The second best position is the Vayu kona(North-West zone).

• Ideally the Temple/Poojasthan should be created at the North-East zone, in case it is not possible, then ensure that the photos and idols of God should be installed on the Poorav disha(East side). The devotee performing Puja should face Poorav disha(East side) or God's photo and moorti should face Poorav disha(East side).

• Install the ‘DIVINE CONNECT VEDIC YANTRA FRAME’ (depicting ShreeYantra, Lord Vastu Devta Vedic Yantra, Lord Kuber Vedic Yantra and Nav Grah Vedic Yantra) in the temple and recite the mantras and worship regularly.

• Play \"Vastu Shanti\" and other mantras in a daily routine.

• Do not keep pictures of departed souls(ancestors) in the Temple and especially on the same platform with other pictures of God. Ideally the pictures of departed souls should be installed on the Dakshin walls.

• Do not transact big and important deals on Amavasya and other inauspicious days.

• Light a Diya in the temple(especially in the morning and at sunset time).

• General attributes of South-East zone(Agni Sthaan):
South-East Ruled by: Lord Agni(the God of Fire).
South-East Ruled by: Venus Planet(Shukra).
Color: Pink/White(Pinkish).
Mantra to be recited: 'Om Dram Drim Droom Sah Shukraey Namah'.
Gemstone: Diamond(Hira).
Element: Fire(Agni Tatva).
