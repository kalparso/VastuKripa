• Never construct a Toilet in the Eshanya Kona(North-East zone) because it could create problems in all spheres of life.

• Never construct a toilet below a staircase.

• The toilet door should not be opposite the Bed or Pooja room.

• Pictures/statues, sacred symbols or murals of God should not be installed on toilet common wall.

• Sprinkle Holy Ganga Jal in the entire property in a routine.

• In the toilet, keep a bowl containing 'white rock salt 'saindha namak'. Every week, throw the old salt in the basin/wc pot. Replace the salt every week.

• Install the DIVINE CONNECT VEDIC YANTRA FRAME (depicting ShreeYantra, Lord Vastu Devta Vedic Yantra, Lord Kuber Vedic Yantra and Nav Grah Vedic Yantra) in the temple and recite the mantras and worship regularly.

• Recite the \"Vastu Shanti\" Mantra and Om Namo Shivay Mantra in a routine.
